Ideation
  • What is the name of your app?

    CommunalShop

  • What collaborative activity can people do together using your app?

    Online Shopping

  • What is the ideal number of concurrent users (must be at least 10 but no more than 40)?

    It could be any number of people.

  • What skills do people need in order to do this activity?

    No skill required at all just need to navigate a web page.

  • Is this activity for fun or for something practical?

    This is a practical activity. It will help organize shopping for parties, or other big events. It will also show keep track of what has been purchased and needs to be as well as where to purchase them.

  • How can a person see what everyone else is doing?

    There will be a shopping list that will update in real-time.

  • How can a person see the most recent result of everyone’s collaborative effort?

    They can look at past lists they helped with or created.

  • What can an admin see (i.e., God’s mode)?

    The admin can see all the lists from everyone.
